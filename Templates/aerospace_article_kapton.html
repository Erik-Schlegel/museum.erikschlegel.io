Apollo 11 Command Module Protective Foil Sample

"Kapton" Foil

  • Kapton Foil is a semi-transparent, gold-colored, aluminized polyimide film.
  • Multiple layers of covered the CSM (aka Columbia) and LEM (aka Eagle) crafts.
  • Protected Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins from temperature extremes during the Apollo 11 Mission.
